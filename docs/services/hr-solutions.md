# HR Solutions — Local & International HR Advisory & Documentation

Our HR Solutions service delivers end-to-end HR process consultation and legally compliant workplace documentation for local and international operations. We help organizations design HR processes and produce the employment documentation they need to operate professionally and in compliance with each country’s labor code.

## Core services
- Local and international HR process consultation
  - Design and optimization of HR workflows: onboarding, performance management, disciplinary processes, and offboarding.
  - Cross-border HR strategy and alignment across jurisdictions while maintaining local compliance.
  - Implementation guidance, SOPs, and training for HR teams and people managers.

- Document drafting & review (compliant with local labor codes)
  - Employment contracts (permanent, fixed-term, temporary, contractor agreements).
  - Offer letters, NDAs, IP agreements, secondment and secondment agreements.
  - Employee handbooks and policies (leave, remote work, data protection, anti-discrimination & anti-harassment).
  - Disciplinary documentation (warning letters, PIP templates).
  - Termination letters, settlement agreements, exit documentation, and severance packages.
  - Jurisdiction-specific addenda capturing mandatory statutory clauses and entitlements.

- Compliance, risk identification & mitigation
  - Documents mapped to each country’s labor code and statutory requirements.
  - Risk identification in HR processes with mitigation recommendations.
  - Compliance checklists for statutory filings, notice periods, and mandatory clauses.

## Who delivers
- Senior HR consultants and employment law experts with hands-on HR leadership experience.
- Team includes professionals with master’s degrees (MSc / MBA / LLM) and sector-specific experience.
- We partner with in-country legal counsel when required to confirm statutory compliance.

## Process & deliverables
1. Discovery
   - Stakeholder interviews, document review, and jurisdiction mapping.
2. Design
   - Drafted process flows, SOPs, and sample documentation tailored to operations.
3. Validation
   - Law-by-law review and stakeholder sign-off (and local counsel review where appropriate).
4. Delivery
   - Finalized documents in editable formats (DOCX / ODT), a compliance summary, and optional training.

Typical deliverables:
- Full document package (contracts, warning letters, termination letters, policies).
- SOPs and HR process flowcharts.
- Compliance checklist listing relevant statutory provisions per jurisdiction.
- Implementation plan and recommended timelines.
